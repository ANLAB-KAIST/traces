Data Traces on User-Generated Videos

In this page, we share our traces on user-generated videos for the wider community use. Our traces include meta-information about videos from YouTube and Daum services. We provide snapshot of all videos in some of their video categories. For more information on the traces, please refer to our paper (linked at the bottom of this page).

YouTube

YouTube Entertainment Category

  • Format: url | length | views | ratings | stars
  • Example: /watch?v=abc|01:30|100|5|4.0
  • Description:
    Each line includes the meta-information of a video. The example above indicates that the YouTube video http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc is 1:30 or 90 seconds long (i.e. length) and is viewed 100 times (i.e. views), while 5 users rated this video (i.e. ratings) and the average score of rating is 4.0 (i.e. stars). Some of the fields in our traces may be empty. This indicates that the value is 0 (such as 0 ratings) or the data collection of that field was not available.
  • Download YouTube Ent Trace (collected at Dec 21, 2006, number of videos = 1,687,506)

    YouTube Science & Technology (now called, Howto & DIY) Category

  • Format: url | upload_date | length | user_id | views1 | ratings1 | views2 | comments2 | favorited2 | ratings2 | stars2 | honors2 | links2 | related2
  • Example: watch?v=abcd1234567|January 16, 2007|01:30|mia|100|5|200|10|10|10|4.0|5|10 https://www.myspace.com/::13 https://www.blogspot.com|/watch?v=a /watch?v=b
  • Description:
    Each line includes the meta-information of a video. This means that YouTube video http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abcd1234567, uploaded by user ID mia, has length of 1:30 or 90 seconds. The views1, ratings1 field reflect the number of views and ratings collected at January 15, 2007, which in this example are 100 and 5, respectively. Then after a month at February 14, 2007, meta-information was collected for the same set of videos. Second collection includes wider set of fields including the number of views, comments, favorites, ratings, stars, honors, linking pages and their clicks, and related videos. Linking videos have a format of clicks page_url, concatenated by :: sign. For instance, in the example, 10 clicks were made from myspace.com web site and 13 clicks were made from blogspot.com web site. Finally, related2 shows the list of related selected by YouTube. Note that both linking pages and related videos in our trace only include those shown in the immediate page of the video.
  • Download YouTube Sci Trace (collected at Jan 15, 2007 and then once more at Feb 14, 2007, number of videos = 252,255)


    Daum

  • Format: video_id | upload_date | length | user_id | recommended | views
  • Example: /ClipView.do?clipid=994690&type=chal|06.11.03|322|80757|3|267
  • Description:
    Each line includes the meta-information of a video. The example here indicates that Daum video whose resource URI is /ClipView.do?clipid=994690&type=chal, uploaded by user whose is 80757, has runtime of 322 secs (5:22). The view and recommendation fields show the number of views and recommendations collected at April, 12, 2007, which in this exaple are 267 and 3, respectively.

  • Download Daum Travel Trace (collected at April 12, 2007, number of videos = 9,295)
